specimen.stage.move_to_device

Move the stage to a position under an imaging device. In that position imaging with the device is allowed.

Syntax

move_to_device(imaging_device)

Parameters

imaging_device int The imaging device to move under. You can use the ImagingDevice enumeration to select the device.

Remarks

  • Not all imaging devices have assigned positions for imaging. You can move to optical microscope, navigation camera (NavCam), electron and ion beam devices.
  • To move back from under NavCam or optical microscope to the original location, move either to ImagingDevice.ELECTRON_BEAM or ImagingDevice.ION_BEAM. These two options are equivalent.
  • If the stage is not under NavCam or optical microscope, moving to ImagingDevice.ELECTRON_BEAM or ImagingDevice.ION_BEAM has no effect.
  • Additional conditions must be met before the stage can move to optical microscope. The stage must be tilted to 52° and the OM alignment procedure must have been completed.

Examples


        # Configure view 4 with optical microscope
        microscope.imaging.set_active_view(4)
        microscope.imaging.set_active_device(ImagingDevice.OPTICAL_MICROSCOPE)
        
        # Assuming the stage is tilted to 52°
        # Move the stage under the optical microscope to enable imaging
        microscope.specimen.stage.move_to_device(ImagingDevice.OPTICAL_MICROSCOPE)
        
        # Take one image by integrating 8 consecutive frames
        settings = GrabFrameSettings(frame_integration=8)
        image = microscope.imaging.grab_frame(settings)
        
        # Move the stage back to the primary beam position
        # Note that this will not tilt the stage to 0 degrees
        microscope.specimen.stage.move_to_device(ImagingDevice.ELECTRON_BEAM)
